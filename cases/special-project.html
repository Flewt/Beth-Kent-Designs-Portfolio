Overview

How can I use Twitch and emotional design to delight a Twitch audience while streaming video games?

The Problem

Many streams rely on functional overlays and standard interactions but lack intentional emotional design strategies that create a sense of delight, surprise, and connection. Without these moments of emotional engagement, audiences may lose interest, switch streams, or fail to form long-term loyalty.

The Challenge

"How might emotional design principles be applied within Twitch streaming experiences to create memorable, engaging, and delightful moments that strengthen the bond between streamer and audience?"

The Solution

Design a brand story that will delight audiences.

Interviews

Interviews conducted with frequent Twitch users to gauge what they felt was most engaging when watching streams.

Highlight Messages

Chat messages highlighted on screen increased the chatter's feeling of connectedness and engagement.

Chat should be visable

Interviewees felt that when they engaged with the stream, it was important to be able to see the message show up on the overlay.

Reflections & Learnings

This project was an exciting opportunity to work at the intersection of things I love: video games, streaming, and design.

I loved getting to explore emotional design as a tool to delight users and audiences. I now firmly believe that emotional design is a core part of UX design, and I'm excited to take the experience I've gained from this project into my future UX work.
